About GDGs

Google Developer Groups

GDGs are community-driven groups for professional developers that want to learn and connect in-person and virtually. They often partner with local companies, organizations, Google Developer Experts (GDEs), and industry experts to provide valuable resources and networking opportunities.

Audience
Professional developers, students, founders, and curious builders.
Events
Groups tailor their activities to their local developer community and host events like:
</>

Workshops

Hands-on coding sessions where developers build real projects, learn new frameworks, and get guided practice with the latest Google technologies.

→

Conferences

Large-scale multi-track events featuring keynotes, breakout sessions, and networking opportunities with hundreds of local developers.

→

Speaker sessions

Expert-led presentations covering topics from machine learning and cloud computing to mobile development and web performance.

→

AMAs

Ask Me Anything sessions with Google Developer Experts and industry leaders, offering direct access to insights and career guidance.

→

Design sprints

Intensive collaborative sessions where teams rapidly prototype and validate ideas, combining design thinking with technical implementation.

→

Meetups

Casual community gatherings for developers to share knowledge, demo side projects, and build connections within their local tech ecosystem.

→
